About MyCGPACalculator.in

Built by a student, for students - providing clean, ad-free CGPA calculators

Why I Created This Website

Hi, I'm Sarath, a student just like you. During my college journey, I frequently needed to calculate my CGPA, convert it to percentage, and track my attendance. Like most students, I turned to Google for help.

What I found was frustrating:

  • Overwhelming advertisements - Websites were cluttered with ads that made it difficult to focus on calculations
  • Poor user interface - Confusing layouts that required multiple clicks to get simple results
  • Inaccurate calculations - Many calculators didn't account for university-specific formulas
  • No mobile optimization - Most sites didn't work well on phones, which is how most students access them
  • Hidden features - Some required registration or payment for basic features

I realized that students needed something better - a clean, simple, and accurate tool that just works. That's when I decided to create MyCGPACalculator.in.

"I wanted to create a website that I would actually want to use - no ads, no distractions, just accurate calculators that help students focus on what matters: their education."

Our Mission

MyCGPACalculator.in exists to provide Indian university students with:

  • Accurate Calculations: Using official university formulas and credit structures
  • Zero Advertisements: A completely ad-free experience so you can focus on your calculations
  • Clean Interface: Simple, intuitive design that works on any device
  • Free Forever: All calculators are free with no hidden charges or premium features
  • Privacy First: All calculations happen in your browser - we don't store any data
  • Regular Updates: Keeping up with latest university regulations and syllabus changes

What Makes Us Different

Unlike generic calculators, we provide pre-configured calculators for specific universities like Anna University, VIT, SRM, and KTU. Each calculator includes:

  • Department-specific course structures
  • Pre-filled credit values based on official regulations
  • Accurate conversion formulas recognized by each university

2. Student-Centric Design

Every feature is designed with students in mind:

  • One-page calculators that don't require navigation
  • Instant results without page reloads

We offer 12 different calculators covering all student needs:

  • University-specific CGPA calculators (Anna, VIT, SRM, KTU)
  • Universal CGPA calculator for 50+ universities
  • SGPA to CGPA conversion
  • CGPA to percentage (50+ university formulas)
  • Percentage to CGPA conversion
  • Attendance calculators with leave planning

4. No Compromises on Quality

We maintain high standards:

  • Regular testing to ensure calculation accuracy
