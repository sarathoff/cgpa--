How to Calculate CGPA at SRM University

CGPA Calculation Formula

Formula: CGPA = (Sum of SGPA × Credits for each semester) ÷ Total Credits

Example:
Semester 1: SGPA = 8.5, Credits = 20
Semester 2: SGPA = 9.0, Credits = 22
CGPA = (8.5 × 20 + 9.0 × 22) ÷ (20 + 22) = 8.76

Step-by-Step Calculation Guide

  1. Collect SGPA: Get your SGPA for each completed semester from your grade sheet
  2. Note Credits: Record the total credits for each semester (usually 18-24 credits)
  3. Multiply: Multiply each semester's SGPA by its total credits
  4. Sum Up: Add all the products from step 3
  5. Divide: Divide the sum by total credits across all semesters
  6. Round: Round the result to two decimal places

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: What is a good CGPA at SRM University?
A CGPA of 8.0 and above is considered excellent at SRM. 7.0-7.9 is good, 6.0-6.9 is above average. Most top companies require a minimum of 7.0-7.5 CGPA for campus placements.
Q2: How is SGPA different from CGPA at SRM?
SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is your GPA for a single semester, while CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the weighted average of all your SGPAs across all completed semesters.
Q3: Can I improve my CGPA in later semesters?
Yes, you can improve your CGPA by performing better in later semesters. However, early semesters have more impact due to cumulative calculation. Focus on high-credit courses for maximum improvement.
Q4: What happens if I fail a course at SRM?
If you fail a course (grade F), you get 0 grade points for that course, which negatively impacts your SGPA and CGPA. You must retake the course in the next available semester or supplementary exam.
Q5: How many credits are required to graduate from SRM?
For B.Tech programs, you typically need to complete 160-165 credits across 8 semesters. This includes core courses, electives, labs, projects, and internship.
Q6: Does SRM have grade improvement option?
Yes, SRM allows students to improve their grades through supplementary exams or by retaking courses. Check with your department for specific policies and procedures.
Q7: What is the minimum CGPA required to avoid detention at SRM?
Students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 5.0 to avoid academic detention. Additionally, 75% attendance is mandatory. Check your regulation handbook for specific requirements.
Q8: How does SRM calculate internal marks?
Internal marks (typically 40-50 marks) are calculated from continuous assessments including mid-term exams, assignments, quizzes, and attendance. These are added to end-semester exam marks for final grade.
Q9: Can I take extra credits to improve CGPA?
Yes, you can take additional elective courses or audit courses to improve your CGPA. However, there may be limits on maximum credits per semester. Consult your academic advisor.
Q10: What CGPA is required for higher studies after SRM?
For M.Tech in India, most IITs/NITs require 6.5-7.0 CGPA. For MS abroad, top universities prefer 8.0+ CGPA. For MBA, 7.0+ CGPA is competitive along with good entrance exam scores.
